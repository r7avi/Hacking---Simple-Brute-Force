How to protect your account?

  • Use long and complex passwords (mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols).
  • Never use common or easily guessable passwords like 123456, password, or your name.
  • Use a reputable password manager to generate and store secure passwords.
  • Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Monitor your accounts for suspicious login activity.

How long do passwords take to crack?

  • 123456 or password — seconds to minutes by brute force.
  • Simple passwords with letters and numbers — minutes to hours.
  • Long, complex passwords (12+ chars with symbols) — thousands of years on modern hardware.
